# ToonExpo Module: Buyer / Visitor Area

## Status

v1

## Purpose

Buyer / Visitor Area is for people who visit ToonExpo, browse projects, save interest and use QR.

## In Scope

- buyer profile;
- name, phone, email;
- personal permanent QR;
- favorites;
- saved projects/apartments;
- sent requests;
- interest/contact history with builders;
- entrance/check-in identity;
- QR interactions with builders.

## Buyer Request / Interest History

Buyer should see records of builder contact/request interactions.

This includes:

- buyer manually sent request from project/apartment page;
- builder scanned buyer QR and created CRM deal/request;
- buyer showed QR and builder saved contact for follow-up.

The builder sees this as a CRM deal/request.

The buyer sees this as request/interest/contact history.

## Registration Rule

Buyer / ordinary visitor can self-register.

BigProjects admin/staff can also create buyer accounts if needed.

Visitor can be registered at event entrance if needed.

## Out Of Scope

- paid ticket wallet;
- payment history.
