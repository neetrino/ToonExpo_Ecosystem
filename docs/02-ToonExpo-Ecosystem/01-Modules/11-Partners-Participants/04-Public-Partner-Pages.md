# Public Partner Pages

## Public Partners List

Public site can show a partner/participant list.

Recommended filters:

- partner type;
- search by name;
- featured partners optional.

## Partner Card

Show:

- logo;
- company name;
- type;
- short description;
- public link/detail action.

## Partner Detail Page

Show:

- company name;
- logo;
- cover image optional;
- type;
- description;
- services/offers;
- contacts/links if public;
- related bank offer section if partner is bank and has published offer.

## Bank Partner Detail

Bank detail can show:

- bank profile;
- bank offer summary;
- link to mortgage calculator page;
- selected bank offer if relevant.

The full calculator belongs to Mortgage / Bank Offers.

## Visibility Rule

Only published partner profiles appear publicly.

Draft/archived partners are hidden from public pages.

## Mobile UX

Partner pages should be mobile-friendly:

- clean cards;
- readable contact/links;
- simple filter;
- no heavy dashboard UI.
