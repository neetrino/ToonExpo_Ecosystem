# Definition And Boundaries

## Definition

Partner/Participant is a non-builder company or organization participating in ToonExpo.

It can appear on public pages and may have an account inside ToonExpo.

## Partner Is Not Builder

Builder participants own real estate projects/apartments.

Partners usually do not own project inventory.

Partners can provide:

- financing;
- technology;
- services;
- sponsorship;
- legal/insurance/support;
- event participation.

## What This Module Owns

Partners / Participants owns:

- partner company profile;
- partner type;
- logo/media;
- public description;
- contacts;
- links;
- generic services/offers;
- public partner list/detail;
- partner account access when enabled.

## What This Module Does Not Own

This module does not own:

- mortgage calculator logic;
- bank offer calculation;
- service provider directory used for readiness;
- builder project inventory;
- CRM sales pipeline.

## Bank Boundary

Banks are partners with a special bank extension.

Bank offer/calculator behavior belongs to Mortgage / Bank Offers.

Partner profile still belongs here.

## v1 Simplicity

v1 should support:

- admin-created partner accounts;
- public partner profile;
- type-specific fields where needed;
- bank offer extension for banks.

Do not build complex partner portals or workflows until needed.
