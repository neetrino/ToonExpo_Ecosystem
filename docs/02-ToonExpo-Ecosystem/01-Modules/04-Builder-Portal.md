## In Scope

- company profile;
- builder users/team;
- projects;
- buildings;
- floors;
- apartments;
- media;
- visual map/hotspots;
- public publication status;
- requests/leads;
- Constructor CRM access;
- Builder Readiness access;
- documents/attachments related to builder data.

## Access Rule

Builders receive access after they become approved/paid ToonExpo participants.

Builder accounts are created by BigProjects Admin/staff or through BOS provisioning. Builders do not self-register in v1.

## UX Rule

Builder should feel that they are inside one ToonExpo portal, not jumping through unrelated platforms.

Main work pages can be boards, lists, maps or management screens. Entity details open in a side sheet over the current context unless the entity is a real long-session workspace.
