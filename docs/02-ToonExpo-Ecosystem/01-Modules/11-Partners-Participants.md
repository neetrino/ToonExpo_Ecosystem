# ToonExpo Module: Partners / Participants

## Status

v1

## Purpose

Partners / Participants represents non-builder companies participating in ToonExpo.

## Types

- banks;
- IT companies;
- sponsors;
- suppliers;
- service providers;
- insurance;
- legal;
- design/furniture;
- other.

## Important Bank Rule

Banks are still partners/participants, but bank partners have special offer fields.

Bank offers appear on the Mortgage / Bank Offers page and affect the public calculator.

## In Scope

- partner listing;
- partner detail page;
- logo;
- description;
- contacts;
- links;
- offers/services;
- basic admin/editing if enabled;
- bank offer form for bank partners;
- type-specific partner forms if needed.

## Out Of Scope

- partner readiness in MVP;
- complex mortgage application flow in MVP.

## Important Rule

Banks are one participant type. The platform should not be built only around banks.

Partner accounts are created by BigProjects Admin/staff or through BOS account creation signal. Partners do not self-register publicly in v1.
