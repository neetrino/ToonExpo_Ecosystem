# ToonExpo MVP Scope Freeze

## Included In v1

- account/access and roles;
- buyer self-registration with name, phone, email;
- permanent buyer QR;
- public website and mobile-like UX;
- builder portal;
- project/building/floor/apartment inventory;
- visual map/hotspots;
- CRM lead intake and Constructor CRM;
- readiness;
- partners/participants and bank offers;
- mortgage calculator page;
- service provider directory;
- exhibition venue map and check-in;
- admin/content/publication controls;
- analytics summaries;
- BOS provisioning endpoint/status.

## Explicitly Out Of Scope

- BigProjects BOS internal CRM/deals/tasks/KPI;
- paid tickets;
- payment/e-ticket flow;
- ticket QR;
- heavy moderation queue;
- full marketplace/ecommerce;
- public chat/messaging;
- broad ToonExpo data sync to BOS;
- complex builder subroles in v1;
- phone/email verification in v1.

## v1 Success Definition

Buyer can register, get QR, browse projects/apartments, request/contact builders, and builders/admins can manage inventory, CRM follow-up and readiness inside ToonExpo.
